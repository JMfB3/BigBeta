BigBeta

BigBeta is a big data multi-product. If you can't appreciate the pun, then idk man

We have three products in the works:

Short interest in the stock market

Our short interest product combines a couple popular approaches to short/mid term investing - Short squeezes and momentum trading. We're constantly monitoring the top gainers in the market - Pre Market, One Day, and After Market, and providing the current data points that are known to be indicators of high potential to move up fast. We're also working on an alerting system for these stocks, as well as auto-buying and paper trading features.

Tracking gym progress for rock climbing

A product to help you track grades that you're sending in the gym. We're working on ways to crowd source routes and add attributes so that we recommend training programs best suited to your weaknesses

Colorado 14er planning and recommendations

Still unreleased, the BigBeta CO14er product aims to help you plan your mountaineering adventures by recommending trails based on multiple altitude weather sources

Hope you like what you see!
